# UI/UX Design Services

Our UI/UX design services blend aesthetic excellence with user psychology to create digital experiences that delight and convert. We begin with research, validate assumptions, and design interfaces that are easy to use, inclusive, and aligned with your business outcomes.

## What We Deliver

### User-Centered Design

We identify user needs, jobs, and pain points, then translate them into intuitive flows and interfaces that reduce friction.

### Responsive Design

Designs that adapt across desktop, tablet, and mobile so customers get a consistent experience wherever they are.

### Accessibility

We design with WCAG principles to make your product usable by people with diverse abilities and to reduce compliance risk.

### Research and Validation

We run interviews, usability tests, and heuristic reviews to validate design decisions and de-risk development.

### Prototyping

Interactive prototypes allow stakeholders to experience the product early and align on direction before development starts.

## Design Toolkit

- **Research**: Interviews, journey mapping, usability testing
- **Design**: Figma, design systems, component libraries
- **Accessibility**: WCAG guidelines, contrast checks, keyboard flows
- **Handoff**: Developer-ready specs, tokens, and interaction notes

## Implementation Stack

When we move from design to build, we deliver with a pragmatic stack:

- **Backend**: Django, Ruby on Rails, Golang, Symfony, Laravel
- **Frontend**: Nuxt, Vue, HTMX, Alpine
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Data**: Postgres
- **Runtime**: Bun

## Outcomes You Can Expect

1. **Higher conversion** with clearer paths and fewer drop-offs
2. **Lower support load** through intuitive experiences
3. **Faster delivery** with clearer design direction
4. **Consistent branding** across screens and touchpoints
5. **Inclusive experiences** that serve a wider audience

## How We Work

1. **Discovery**: Goals, users, and success metrics
2. **Research**: Evidence gathering and synthesis
3. **Design**: Flows, wireframes, and UI system
4. **Validation**: Testing with real users
5. **Handoff**: Design system + implementation support
