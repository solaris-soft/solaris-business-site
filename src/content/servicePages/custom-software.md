# Custom Software Development

Our custom software development services create purpose-built applications tailored to your exact processes and goals. We design around your workflow so your team works faster and with fewer compromises.

## What We Build

### Bespoke Applications

Unique solutions that fit your business model rather than generic tools you have to adapt to.

### Scalable Architecture

Designed to grow with your business in users, data, and features.

### Industry-Specific Expertise

We build with compliance, process, and domain requirements in mind.

### Legacy System Integration

Modern solutions that integrate cleanly with existing systems.

### Ongoing Support

We keep your software secure, reliable, and evolving over time.

## Why Custom Software

1. **Exact fit** to your processes
2. **Better adoption** by internal teams
3. **Less manual work** and fewer workarounds
4. **Future-ready** architecture

## Technology Stack

- **Backend**: Django, Ruby on Rails, Golang, Symfony, Laravel
- **Frontend**: Nuxt, Vue, HTMX, Alpine
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Data**: Postgres
- **Runtime**: Bun

## Delivery Approach

1. **Discovery**: Map current workflows and goals
2. **Design**: Define the ideal process and system
3. **Build**: Agile delivery with regular demos
4. **Launch**: Training, support, and continuous improvement
