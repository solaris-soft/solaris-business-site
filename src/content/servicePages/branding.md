# Branding Services

Our branding services create compelling identities that resonate with your target audience and differentiate you in crowded markets. We focus on clarity, consistency, and credibility across every touchpoint.

## What We Build

### Brand Strategy

We define your positioning, audience, competitive landscape, and the core story your brand should tell.

### Visual Identity

Logos, typography, color systems, and visual assets that express your personality and values.

### Brand Messaging

Value propositions, tone of voice, and key messages that sound like you and speak to your market.

### Brand Consistency

Guidelines and templates that keep your brand aligned across web, print, and product.

### Brand Experience

A cohesive experience across marketing, product, and customer support that builds trust.

## Deliverables

- **Brand foundation**: mission, values, positioning
- **Visual system**: logo suite, colors, typography, imagery
- **Messaging kit**: elevator pitch, taglines, key messages
- **Guidelines**: usage rules and examples

## Digital Implementation Stack

When branding is implemented in product or web, we build with:

- **Backend**: Django, Ruby on Rails, Golang, Symfony, Laravel
- **Frontend**: Nuxt, Vue, HTMX, Alpine
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Data**: Postgres
- **Runtime**: Bun

## Why It Matters

1. **Recognition**: People remember you faster
2. **Trust**: Consistency reduces perceived risk
3. **Alignment**: Internal teams move in one direction
4. **Differentiation**: Clear reason to choose you

## Our Process

1. **Discovery**: Business goals and market context
2. **Strategy**: Positioning and messaging
3. **Design**: Visual identity and assets
4. **System**: Guidelines and rollout plan
