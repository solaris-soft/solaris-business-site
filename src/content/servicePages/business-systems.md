# Business Systems Services

Our business systems services create integrated operational environments that unify data and processes across departments. We help you move from disconnected tools to a cohesive ecosystem that improves efficiency and visibility.

## What We Implement

### ERP Solutions

Centralize finance, inventory, HR, and operations in one source of truth.

### CRM Implementation

Customer relationship systems that support sales, marketing, and service teams.

### Business Intelligence

Dashboards and reporting that turn data into decisions.

### Workflow Management

Digitize complex internal processes with automated handoffs.

### Cloud Migration

Move legacy systems to secure, scalable cloud infrastructure.

## Benefits

1. **Unified data** across teams
2. **Faster decisions** with real-time insights
3. **Reduced manual work** and errors
4. **Better customer experiences**

## Technology Stack

- **Backend**: Django, Ruby on Rails, Golang, Symfony, Laravel
- **Frontend**: Nuxt, Vue, HTMX, Alpine
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Data**: Postgres
- **Runtime**: Bun

## Delivery Approach

1. **Assessment**: Systems audit and gap analysis
2. **Architecture**: Integration and data flow design
3. **Implementation**: Phased rollout with validation
4. **Enablement**: Training and documentation
