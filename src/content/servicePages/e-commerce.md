# E-Commerce Solutions

Our e-commerce solutions combine compelling user experiences with robust backend systems to drive sales and customer loyalty. We design every touchpoint to reduce friction and increase conversion.

## What We Build

### Custom E-Commerce Platforms

Branded storefronts that reflect your business and differentiate you from competitors.

### Payment Processing

Secure, frictionless checkout with multiple payment options.

### Inventory Management

Sync online and offline inventory with real-time visibility.

### Mobile Commerce

Mobile-first shopping experiences that perform on any device.

### Conversion Optimization

Data-driven improvements to reduce abandonment and grow average order value.

### Shopify Implementations

When Shopify is the right fit, we deliver custom setups with tailored themes, integrations, and merchandising workflows.

## Technology Stack

- **Backend**: Django, Ruby on Rails, Golang, Symfony, Laravel
- **Frontend**: Nuxt, Vue, HTMX, Alpine
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Data**: Postgres
- **Runtime**: Bun
- **Commerce**: Custom Shopify builds and integrations
